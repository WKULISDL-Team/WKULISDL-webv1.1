Mission


Wolkite university library and information service is central to the intellectual life of the university. The library supports, enhances,and collaborates in the education,research and service activities of the university by:-
 Ensuring that collage, students, and other users have access to a broad and diversified range of scholarly resources, both through its own rich collection of materials and through its collaborations with other institutions;
 Collecting, organizing, disseminating, and preserving rare and unique scholarly and institutional resources for the use of scholars and researchers
 Supporting and collaborating with collage in teaching, research, and practice;
 Conducting original research that contributes to the overall understanding of library and information science; andengaging in outreach to the community through a process of continuous evaluation and improvement.

User service


Is one of library service which has direct contact with users.
In academic library public services are provide information in different format to the users. Public service is categorized
circulation section, reference, periodicals, documentation, internet service etc. In circulation where you find book issuing and returning system. Circulation function may allow users to borrowing books out of the library for a specified period of time.



Documentation and Periodicals Service


Documentation and periodicals service Documentations and periodicals are offered mostly on spot reading basis because they are not meant for loan outside the library. The services performed at this section are:-

  Organizing documents in a systematic manner and produce appropriate record.
 Prepare index for all document.
 Prepare comprehensive index for all periodical title which the library has received.
 Prepare index to journal articles on education and related subject orientation.
  Handle clipping on education and related subjects.
  Collecting research papers on education by our university.
 Assists research in getting documents.
 Instructors and researchers may be granted to borrow periodicals and documentations for not more than a week.

In periodical and documentation section only the staff members and students can have an access and the materials are not loan.

Rules of the library


Library members and visitors should respect the rights of another library user at all times by not participating in behavior likely to intervene within the convenience of another library user.
The following general rules and regulations should be respected with regards to the use of the library Smoking is strictly forbidden in the library environment. Switch off cell phones. Silence should be observed. Group discussion & moving chairs and the like from place to place is not allowed. Illegal possession of library materials, transfer of ID, or pocket from person to person is not permitted.
